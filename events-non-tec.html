PLACEMENT FEVER

About:- This event puts the students through a simulated version of the campus placement process. Beginning with an aptitude test, the participants will go on to appear for group discussions and eventually sit for personal interviews.
Rounds:-
The event will be organized in three rounds. All three rounds will be conducted at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad.
Round 1:
The format of the Curriculum Vitae will be provided immediately after registration which participants have to fill sincerely and proceed further. The preliminary round will be an Aptitude and English Proficiency Test round.
Round 2:
The next challenge is the Group Discussion (GD) round, where participants will be divided into a certain number of groups to discuss certain topics, which will be provided on the spot.
Round 3:
And the final round is the Personal Interview. Both HR & Technical interviews will be conducted in a simulated environment.
Judging Criteria:
First of all, participants will be shortlisted on the basis of the curriculum vitae.
The Aptitude and English Proficiency Test and the Group Discussion will be the elimination round through which participants will be selected for the Personal Interview.
The 2-5 participants will get a certificate of merit; however, each of the participants will receive the certificate of participation.
Any misbehavior would automatically lead to disqualification.

Modal Dialog

Global Offensive, like prior games in the Counter-Strike series, is an objective-based, multiplayer first-person shooter. Two opposing teams, known as the Terrorists and the Counter Terrorists, compete in game modes to complete objectives, such as securing a location to plant or defuse a bomb and rescuing or guarding hostages.At the end of each round, players are rewarded based on their individual performance with in-game currency to spend on more powerful weapons in subsequent rounds. Winning rounds results in more money than losing, and completing objectives such as killing enemy players gives cash bonuses.

  • Participation fee will be Rs. 10 per head.
  • A team of 4 members is required that you can form by your side or individuals will be grouped at the time of event.
  • Everyone needs to bring their own laptop and mobile phone for creating hotspot.
  • Supervisors will be there for every team.
  • 3 teams will be selected based on knockout. Five winners will be selected based on no of kills in Headshot round.
