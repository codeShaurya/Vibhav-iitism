PIRATES OF CIRCUIT

This is a modal window. You can do the following things with it:

Ever dreamt of being bombarded by a stream of resistors, diodes, capacitors and inductors? If yes, then it’s your time. Potray your talents and show us what these elements are capable of. "PIRATES OF CIRCUITS " an event which will scrutinize the Ohm, Farad and Henry with in you and will hallmark you as the king of circuits, if you emerge as the winner. Practical knowledge combined with what you learned within the walls of the classroom is what you require to attain the ultimate glory.
So let the frenzy, furores and fuss of the circuits run through your veins and prepare yourself not to get ELECTROCUTED!
Theme:
The theme of this event " PIRATES OF CIRCUITS " is ELECTIONS.As every election becomes a heated topic to discuss in the society, it is time to incorporate your interest in electrical and electronics on this election theme. To participate in this event all one needs is basic knowledge about electrical and electronics (mainly digital) concepts.
Problem Statement:
Using electrical and electronic concepts to decode sequences and build the circuits.
Round 1:
Here the participant will be given story where every character in the story will be described with respect to electrical components' characteristics. The participant has to identify what these characters corresponds to in electrical elements. And then connect the identified elements in the particular sequence, as mentioned, on a bread board. And has to identify across which element the given LED bulb will glow.
.EXAMPLE:
Each character represents an electrical element. The behavior of the person corresponds to the element's characteristics.Using these as clues identify what each character is in electrical analogy.
Character A: Doesn't think of the past. He thinks only of the future.
Character B: Adopts and responds immediately to all situations.
Character C: Doesn't like changes in life.
Relate these clues to the elements' behavior and identify these characters. Then, connect these elements so that there is no current division between them on the given bread board. Now, identify across which element the LED will glow. ( You will be provided with a 12V battery)
Round 2:
The final round is called ‘The Stock Game’.
Three teams will be participating in this round.
A circuit will be given to each team containing four resistances and a voltage source. In this stock game, three resistor manufacturing companies will set their base price for different values of resistance.
Initially all three teams will be given 30 virtual rupees. Then each team will sell or buy their stock based on market value of resistance of particular company. At the end money left with each team shows the value of voltage source they will use in their circuit.
Each team should have at least 4 resistors with them at the end of game. Finally based on the circuit given in starting they will connect voltages and resistor and will calculate power
Team with max power will win the electrohunt.
JUDGING CRITERIA:
Judging for the FIRST round is based on the number of identified elements and how close the obtained output is to the desired criteria. In the second round, judgment will be based on the method that has been adopted to complete the circuit and to display the output. Rules and Regulations:-
* The participants must have a valid college ID.
* Mobiles and calculators should not be used during the event.
* In case of any fraud or cheating, the participant will be disqualified from the competition.
* Organizers reserve the right to change rules as they consider fit.
* Decision of the judges will be final and binding to all.

Robo soccer

Robo soccer
An event to showcase  your robotics talent and  also  to  bring    the  football  spirit.  Participants  are  required  to build  manually  controlled  bot  capable of  playing  soccer  on  an  arena specially  designed  for  the  robotic  soccer  match.Do you have it in you to make your bots play and win the soccer cup?
Rules :
Max participants in a group:4
1)Each team has to build one wired/wireless bot that can defend as well as score goal on the opposite side by hitting ,dragging or pushing the ball.
2)Two halves of 4 minutes each will be given and extra time of one minute will be given in case of a tie.
3) Each team can have a maximum of 4 team members.
4) The weight limit of the robot for this competition is 4 Kg.
5) Each robot should not exceed by maximum 30cmx30cmx30cm even after any extension.
6) A team can make either a wired or a wireless bot each within the
above dimensions and weight limit.
7) The ball can be hit, dragged or pushed by the team (Teams are not allowed to make contraptions for holding or lifting the ball. Grippers are not allowed).
8)The voltage at any point on the electrical connections should not exceed 12 V DC.
9)In case of a tie 1 minute extra time will be given to the teams.
10) The robots are not allowed to use grippers or actuations which are intended to harm the opponent’s robot.
Judging criteria:
The team with maximum number of goals wins the match. The team that wins through all the knockout stages wins the robosoccer cup.

Pace Line follower-

Robotics is taken to the next level when the robots take their decisions of their own.In this event , an autonomous bot has to be made that can follow a wide strip of black line dodging all the difficulties coming its way at brisk pace. The fastest one to complete the track gets to win the pace line follower
Rules:
Max participants in a group:4
1.In the first round, the track is to be traversed completely by the bot to qualify to the next round. If the bot leaves the track , the team stands disqualified . A team can be given three chances at maximum . Timing will not be considered for the round one.
2. In the second round, the fastest bot to complete the track wins the event
3. Furthermore, the bot may have to pass through bridges and tunnels each of width 150 m
4 . The width of the line is 25 mm .
5. The arena includes black straight lines and curves. It’s dimensions are 6 ft x 5 ft.
6. At one specific point, the line is discontinuous. BOT SPECIFICATION
• The size of the bot must be less than 200X150X100 mm in size. Care must be taken so that the bot fits in the arena.
• The bot may have any number of wheels.
• Use of internal combustion engines in the bot is not allowed.
• The bot may have its own source of power.
• External power source of 220 Volts AC will be provided.
• Teams are permitted to implement their own choice of steering mechanism.
• The bot should be capable of climbing slopes and going down descends.
r • The bot should be able to find its way back on the track
• The bot should be able to find its way back on the track where the line is discontinuous.
• Teams are allowed to use readymade micro controllers and board or sensor kit.
• Teams are not allowed to use readymade kits or any such assemblies.
Judging criteria:
Ranking will be on the basis on timing of second round. The teams with least time will be declared winner and rest teams will be ranked accordingly .
